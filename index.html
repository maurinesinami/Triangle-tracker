Triangle Tracker

A triangle tracker is an online app that determines the type of a triangle.To use this app you should insert your three values as asked then hit the ok button and you get your result.Lets breifly discuss about triangles:

Triangles

A triangle is a shape with three sides. We have three types of triangles:

  • Equilateral.
  • isosceles.
  • Scalene.

Equilateral

This is a triangle which hat all the sides equal.

For example:

  • side a =23
  • side b=23
  • side c=23

Isosceles

This is a triangle that has only two sides that are equal.

For example:

  • side a=23 .
  • side b=23.
  • side c=24.

Scalene

This is a triangle whose all sides are not equal.

For example:

  • side a=23
  • side b=24
  • side c=25
